  • The importance of the Internet of Things

    • Internet of Things use:
    Communications: It is worth noting that the telecommunications sector is most affected and involved in the Internet of Things. As the latter will play an effective and significant role in preserving all data related to this matter, according to which all smart devices of all kinds, whether personal computers or smart phones, are required to maintain a reliable connection with the Internet to ensure business continuity with high efficiency.
    Health care: The health care field is considered one of the largest areas where the Internet of things can be applied, as patients can be monitored remotely and their cases recorded in electronic records, and it can also be activated in wearable devices to impose control on exercise and the sleep style of patients and other health habits, in addition to To the above It can contribute to saving many lives when reporting a pre-existing risk to the patient's health before death.
    Retail: The beneficiary is considered in this context from the Internet of Things both the consumer and the merchant at the same time, where the merchant monitors the goods available to him in the store and the operations of buying and selling, while the consumer is provided with the latest provided goods that interest him in the stores that he used to .
    Factories and companies: The industrial sector is a sector that has abundant capabilities in integrating IOT in its dimensions, and this may be represented by attracting data on machines in the factory sections and the accumulated materials and equipment on the shelves in warehouses, and the role of the Internet of Things here is to track the available resources in terms of the possibility of their penetration and problems And others; This entails ensuring that the business is performed efficiently and effectively and that costs that could have been spent on risks were saved.
