    The scope of application of IoT technology is very broad in everyday life, and the most important of these examples and applications:

    Applications from the Internet of Things:
    Smart and wearable devices.
    Detecting fumes rising nearby, and siren accordingly.
    Disclosing the inventory of manufacturing materials, for example, by notifying what is nearing completion.
    Automatic arrangement of people's daily appointments.
    Informing of a potential hazard in an environment.
    Monitor the availability of fuel in heating stations and alert in the event of completion.
