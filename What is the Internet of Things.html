    • What is the Internet of Things?

    It is referred to in the world of technology as IOT, a modern technical
    method that aims to attract things represented by devices and sensors
    and connect them to the Internet to transmit data between them without
    human intervention, and that is automatic if the thing is in the geographical
    area covered by the Internet
